Operational memory layer

Your company's memory, kept where it belongs.

Certemis is the operational memory layer for companies. It captures how your business actually works — the decisions, context and know-how that normally live only in people's heads — and gives it back to your team the moment they need it.

The problem

Most of what your company knows is never written down.

It lives in conversations, inboxes and the heads of a few experienced people. The day they're unavailable — on holiday, between projects, or gone for good — recovering that context costs real time and invites expensive mistakes.

What Certemis is

A memory that stays, even when people move on.

Not another wiki to write and maintain. Certemis captures context from the tools you already use and turns it into answers — current, in plain language, and limited to what each person is allowed to see.
